Why FraudBnB?

Vacation rental scams have been prevalent since well before the pandemic, so why havenâ€™t rental websites such as Airbnb incorporated scam detection into their products?

A team member recently experienced this scenario - the 15 person family arrived at a beautiful Beverly Hills mansion after having paid a large sum of money, only to find out that the mansion, in fact, did not exist. While they got their money back, the situation could have been completely avoided with proper vetting, and the experience was left wanting.

Our product will take into account several factors such as listing reviews, host reviews, analysis of pictures and address verification in order to establish trust and reliability for the consumer renting on Airbnb.

F.A.Q

Here we have answered some of our most frequently asked questions. If your question is not answered here, please reach out to us directly and we would be happy to assist.

  • What areas are currently supported by your product?

    Our dashboard and map currently support listings from the Greater Los Angeles area. However, we are currently working to expand our coverage to other areas all over the world. Feel free to reach out to us directly to inquire about the status of your specific areas of interest.

  • What are the fees associated with your product?

    Our product is risk-free and your company will not be charged if the product does not work for you. We charge a flat rate of 20% of any revenue that your company saves through the use of our fraud detection systems.

  • What does my company get if we sign up to use your services?

    Once you sign up, our engineering team will tailor our baseline algorithms to your data. Your customized models and algorithms will can then be easily integrated into your company's infrastructure. Our technical support team will be readily available to assist you with any problems that may arise.

    In addition, we offer a custom dashboard and interactive map that makes it simple to see the overall health of your company's listings.

  • How do you determine the reliability of a listing?

    We analyze all of your listing reviews, using natural language processing techniques, to determine a reliability score for the specific listing.

  • How can I download a report of my listings?

    The FraudBnB team is working hard on this feature; the option to download reports will be available soon!
